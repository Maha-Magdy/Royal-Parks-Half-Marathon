Royal Parks Half Marathon

2021

If you’ve always dreamt of running the Royal Parks Half Marathon, we've rounded up everything you need to know about this year’s event.

2021.04.11(SUNDAY)

@ route through central London, and four of London’s eight Royal Parks

Main information

Where

The nearest tube station to the start and finish zone is Knightsbridge.
Hyde Park Corner tube is a 15-minute walk away.

When

The rescheduled 2020 race will take place on Sunday, April 11.Owing to coronavirus restrictions, runners are being invited to take part virtually.

How long

The Royal Parks Half Marathon is 13.1 miles and takes you on a route through central London, and four of London’s eight Royal Parks – Hyde Park, The Green Park, St James’s Park.
